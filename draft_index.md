# What's for Dinner? Automation à la Carte

*Turning a daily decision into an automated system*

## The Problem

Every night, my house ran into the same question:

“What’s for dinner?”

And more often than not, you'd forget anything you've ever eaten or anything you've ever liked. It was a werid and frustrating place to find yourself - especially when kids needed to be fed and time wasn't on your side.

So that's when I questioned if this was a decision that could be taken off our plates.
